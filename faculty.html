Our Faculty

The hands behind every cookie

Four bakers, one obsession. Each brings something unique to the kitchen.

Lena Kovač

Head Baker

14 years in pastry. Trained at Le Cordon Bleu Paris. Lena tastes every batch before it ships — she has never approved a cookie she wouldn't feed to her own children.

Marcus Chen

Dough Master

Former bread baker who switched to cookies and never looked back. Marcus manages the 48-hour cold fermentation schedule with the precision of a Swiss watchmaker.

Priya Sharma

Flavor Architect

Priya develops every new flavor. She's tested over 200 chocolate sources and keeps a notebook of rejected combinations. The Raspberry Dark was her idea.

Diego Reyes

Pack & Ship Lead

The last pair of hands on every box. Diego inspects every cookie under a lamp, hand-stamps every box, and has been known to re-pack an order at 5pm because a corner looked off.
